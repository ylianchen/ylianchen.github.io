 YCH 
Y.Liang Chen

The concept came from the idea of the crt TV as a daily space probe, because of its analog nature. It then raises my attention towards the problem in our life with technology:

“What do we do when we want to know something about a black box?

--We ask another black box.”

In this project, a beam of light came from a black box, casted onto a black box, and then captured by another black box in the hands of the user.

By directing the light through objects, I want to explore the dynamic between human and machines. In this project, a beam of light came from a black box, casted onto a black box, and then captured by another black box in the hands of the user.
