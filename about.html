Y.Liang Chen

BIOGRAPHY

Yongliang Chen is a photographer, designer and Audio Visual artist working at media and technology. His artistic practice spans across diverse medium, Through animation, video games, generative art, and installations, he explores the underlying mechanisms between technology, interactivity, and visual storytelling.
He has worked as a freelance street and portrait photographer, 3D CG artist. He was the creative assistant of Hafnia foundation, and now working closely with Institute of creativity and Innovation Xiamen University. He is also cofounder of Cheap universe game studio.
