 YCH 
Y.Liang Chen

The projection mapping project draws inspiration from "The Circular Ruins." I want to explore the complex themes of creation, recursion, and the blurred line between reality and dreams. Using a Kinect sensor, a projector, and a laptop running TouchDesigner, The Kinect sensor captures infrared data, detecting human activity and presence within the space. This data is then analyzed by image generative algorithms through an api, employing the given keyword "dandelion" to craft a visual representation of the human form. Once the generative algorithm shapes the visuals, the resulting imagery is integrated back into TouchDesigner. The reimagined visuals being projected onto a physical wall, mapping the transformed imagery onto the tangible space, unveiling hidden narratives within the human silhouette.
